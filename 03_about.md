I started my career as an artist completing a BA Hons degree in Illustration, I then went on to completing various projects
utilising both drawing and digital techniques within my work. My interest in art and technology developed further when I
completed a Masters in Digital Moving Imagery. Since then I have been able to hone my skills in graphics software and
creative technologies extensively. In 2015 I enhanced my skills considerably by learning how to write code and specifically
web development. As a designer/ developer I understand the importance of excellent functionality working seamlessly with
engaging user experience and beautiful design. With a creative vision I continue to research new emerging technologies to
expand my skills in design and front end development. Aside from my passion in design and development I am an
experienced swimmer, scuba diver, cyclist, black belt kickboxer and photographer who loves to travel.
