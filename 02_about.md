I started my career as an artist completing a BA Hons degree in Illustration. I then went on to completing various projects utilising both drawing and digital techniques within my work. My interest in art and technology developed further when I completed a Masters in Digital Moving Imagery. Since then I have been able to hone my skills in digital tools significantly and have a passion for researching new technologies. I have been using digital software and hardware in recent years and I wanted to develop my skills further by learning code and specificially web development at General Assembly. I have a passion for digital art and giving the user a beautiful visual experience on the web and and am excited to continue to develop my skills in front end web development. Aside from my passion in art, technology and web development I am a keen cyclist, swimmer, scuba diver, black black kickboxer and photographer who loves to travel.
